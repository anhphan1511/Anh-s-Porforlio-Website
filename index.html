Labor Market Outcomes and Minimum Wage Policy

Used panel data from multiple states to analyze the impact of minimum wage changes on employment and earnings. Implemented fixed effects regression models in Stata, controlled for time and state-specific trends, and visualized the estimated effects across industries.

Predicting Housing Prices with Python

Built regression models in Python to predict housing prices using features such as location, size, and local economic indicators. Compared model performance across linear regression, regularized models, and tree-based methods, and evaluated results using cross-validation.

Time Series Analysis of GDP Growth

Analyzed quarterly GDP data to study trends, cycles, and shocks. Estimated ARIMA models, produced short-term forecasts, and interpreted results in the context of macroeconomic events.

Survey-Based Study of Consumer Expectations
